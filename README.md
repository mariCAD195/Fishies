"talk to" - talk to NPC in the room
"search" - search closely a fish tank in the room
"go home" - end the day and come back the next morning
"move" - move to a different room
"open journal" - open journal where you can look at your progress
"backpack" - see what items you have in your inventory
"exit" - exit the game duh
